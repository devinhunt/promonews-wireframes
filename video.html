    About

    The latest video by Krisoffer Borgli, for Oslo’s disco master Todd Terje’s track Inspector Norse features Marius Solem Johansen as the eponymous Inspector, happily grooving in Norwegian suburbia – apart from when he’s consumed his home-cooked drugs…

    Kristoffer has delighted previously with his videos for Casiokids, Young Dreams, The Golden Filter and more, and this video is actually an excerpt of a 15-minute short film called WHATEVEREST, written and directed by Kristoffer, with music by Todd, that’s coming soon.
